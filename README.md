# 🏥 Hospital Emergency Room Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the performance of a Hospital Emergency Room (ER) using Power BI. The goal is to identify operational inefficiencies, improve patient experience, and provide actionable insights for stakeholders.

The dashboard helps in monitoring key metrics such as patient volume, wait time, satisfaction score, and referral patterns to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To enhance emergency room efficiency and patient experience by:
- Reducing patient wait time
- Improving satisfaction levels
- Optimizing resource allocation
- Managing non-critical patient load effectively

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
- Total Number of Patients
- Average Wait Time
- Patient Satisfaction Score (Out of 10)
- Number of Patients Referred
- % of Patients Seen Within 30 Minutes
- Admission vs Non-Admission Rate

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
The dataset contains detailed patient-level information including:

- Patient ID (Unique Identifier)
- Admission Date
- Gender
- Age
- Race
- Wait Time
- Department Referral
- Admission Status
- Satisfaction Score

This data enables both **high-level analysis** and **granular patient-level insights**.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### ⏱️ Wait Time Issue
- Average wait time is ~35 minutes
- ~40% of patients are not treated within 30 minutes

👉 Indicates operational inefficiency in patient handling

---

### 😊 Low Patient Satisfaction
- Average satisfaction score is ~5/10
- Drops further when wait time increases

👉 Strong relationship between delay and patient experience

---

### 🚑 High Non-Critical Patient Load
- ~50% patients are not admitted
- Majority cases do not require referral

👉 ER is being used for minor cases, causing overcrowding

---

### 📅 Peak Load Problem
- Highest patient volume on weekends and Mondays
- Peak hours: 10 AM – 12 PM and evenings

👉 Uneven resource allocation leads to delays

---

### 🏥 Department Insights
- Most patients do not require referral
- General Practice and Orthopedics are top referral departments

👉 Indicates need for better patient routing

---

## 🚨 Problems Identified
- High wait time
- Low patient satisfaction
- Overcrowding in ER
- Inefficient triage system
- Poor peak-time resource allocation

---

## 💡 Recommendations

### 🔹 Improve Wait Time
- Increase staff during peak hours
- Introduce fast-track system for minor cases

---

### 🔹 Implement Triage System
- Categorize patients based on severity
- Prioritize critical cases

---

### 🔹 Reduce ER Overload
- Redirect non-emergency patients to OPD
- Introduce initial screening process

---

### 🔹 Optimize Resource Allocation
- Focus staffing during peak days and hours

---

## 📊 Business Impact

If implemented, these improvements can:
- Reduce patient wait time
- Increase satisfaction score
- Improve operational efficiency
- Enhance hospital reputation
- Optimize resource utilization
